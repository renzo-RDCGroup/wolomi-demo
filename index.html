Two ways to fix Wolomi's lead form

The current eligibility form asks ~18 questions up front — thorough for enrollment, but heavy for cold paid traffic. Here are two friendlier intake concepts that qualify a mom first, then collect the rest.

The idea: capture the 4 things that decide eligibility — county, life-stage, Medi-Cal status, and consented contact — in under 30 seconds. Everything else (race, due date, language, device, etc.) gets collected after she's engaged, by the Mama Companion or a step-2 form. Same data, far less drop-off.

Concept A

Stepped form + progress bar

One question per screen with an encouraging progress bar. Familiar, fast, and high-converting — the proven workhorse for paid lead-gen.

  • Tappable big-button answers
  • Animated progress + microcopy ("Almost there 💛")
  • Instant "you qualify" + next-steps screen

Concept B

Conversational chat intake

Feels like texting a real Mama Companion. Warm and human — fits Wolomi's trust-first brand and the community-health-worker model.

  • Chat bubbles from "Maya," a Mama Companion
  • Typing indicators + progress dots
  • Highest emotional warmth; great for cold social traffic

Concept C · v3

Phone-first + bilingual

Captures the number on step 1 (so an abandoned form is still a lead), in English or Spanish, with trust built in for the SD/Imperial audience.

  • Phone-first partial capture + speed-to-lead promise
  • EN / ES toggle · "Meet your Mama Companion"
  • Immigration-safe reassurance · callback scheduler · welcome gift
